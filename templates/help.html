  • Getting Started

    • When you land on this site, firstly register your account with a valid Email ID.
    For that click on the Signup button on the navigation bar.
    Fill the form with correct details and your account is ready.
    Now, go to login page and enter your email and password and press submit.
    This will take you to verification page and you will receive an OTP at your email ID.
    Enter this OTP in the enter OTP field and click on submit.
    Bravo, you are a verified user now !
    Login to your account and change your details as much as you want.

  • How to autofill

    • Open the website you want to autofill your form with the Application-Bharo tool.
    Click on the autofill extension on the top right corner.
    Enter your email ID and password and click on OK button. You are now logged in.
    Click on Autofill button and your form will get autofilled itself with the data you have already provided in the site.
    Congratulations, your form is AUTOFILLED !
    HAPPY AUTOFILLING :)

  • How to autoupdate

    • When autofilling a form click on the extension and log in.
    Now when you fill any field of the form which you have already not provided at the website, click on the Autoupdate button.
    And done.
    New field and its value is autoupdated.
